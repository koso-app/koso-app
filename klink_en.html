K LINK supports the use of KOSO RX-5 motorcycle meter through Bluetooth connection with Tung Ya, enabling mutual message exchange and transmission. When connected to a mobile phone, KOSO RX-5 can display incoming calls and music titles, as well as use the App for navigation planning and display turn-by-turn instructions and estimated arrival time on the vehicle meter.

K LINK

Motorcycle Dashboard Mobile Navigation

Plan your route through your phone and display route instructions on the vehicle dashboard by exchanging navigation data via Bluetooth. K LINK provides map location search and record functions in the app, where you can save favorite locations and choose navigation options to guide users to their destination. The map allows you to quickly find nearby repair shops, gas stations, and parking lots, making it more convenient to use.
  • Plan your route with Google Maps
  • Provide turn-to-turn navigation instructions
  • Display the next turn distance and estimated arrival time

Real-time Data Capture and Storage

Users can record vehicle maintenance and repair records on the app and automatically record refueling and mileage through Koso instruments, which can be used for automatic reminders for refueling and maintenance and as a reference for future maintenance schedules.
Before setting off, K LINK provides 24-hour weather forecasts, allowing drivers to prepare rain gear in advance and travel with peace of mind. Real-time oil prices are obtained from the internet to provide abundant driving information for early refueling preparation before setting off.

KOSO was officially established in 1989 as an independent brand developed in-house due to the necessity of future market marketing. KOSO was registered as a trademark of Tung Ya Electric Industries Co., Ltd. at that time. The name "KOSO" comes from the Japanese words for "concept" and "high speed". "Concept" means creativity and "high speed" represents high-performance. Combined, they represent the company's hope to use creativity to create high-performance products!
